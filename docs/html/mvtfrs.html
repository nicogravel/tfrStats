On the statistical assessment of multivariate TFRs¶

Background¶

It has been proposed that two frequency bands of electrophysiological brain activity contain the most stimulus information in visual cortex 1,2,3,4,5,6. Therefore, a number of statistical assessments have been suggested 7.
